Vanilla Cake Recipe

This vanilla cake recipe is great for all occasions and easy to make.

Prep time: 15 min

Cook time: 1 hour

Servings: 10

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 22/3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch cake tin with cooking spray and line with parchment paper.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, vanilla extract, and salt together in a large bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and vegetable oil; mix by hand or beat with an electric mixer on low speed until smooth.
  4. Add more flour if batter is too runny. Pour into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30min.
  6. Cool on a wire rack for 5 minutes. Run a table knife around the edges to loosen.
  7. Invert cake carefully onto a cooling rack. Let cool completely.
